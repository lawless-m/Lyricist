[The wink — spoken, flat, cold]

[Verse 1 — clipped, technical, controlled]
Six delivery rooms on this floor, tonight only one door says occupied.
Twelve years in, I know a heartbeat by its static,
know the half-second a room forgets to breathe.
Rostered for three deliveries a shift the year I started —
rostered for one a fortnight now, and I still show up for that.

[Hook — chanted, stacked]

I've delivered four thousand of them, and I never once delivered myself.

Give me the choice again — I'd still take the hallway over the house.

but the cursor blinks in the empty box, and some nights I let it.
